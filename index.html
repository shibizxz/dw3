Common Questions
What investors ask before joining
Is my capital safe with DailyWealth4You?
Yes. Your funds are always held in your own regulated broker account. We manage strategy only — your capital is never transferred to or held by us.
How does the AI signal engine work?
Our AI scans 28 pairs across 6 timeframes in real time, identifying high-probability setups using pattern recognition, RSI, MACD confluence, and volume analysis.
Can I withdraw my funds at any time?
Absolutely. There are no lock-up periods. You can request a withdrawal from your broker account at any time without penalties or restrictions of any kind.
What returns can I realistically expect?
Target is 6–10% monthly on Discretionary Growth mandate. Historical averages support this range. Returns vary with market conditions and are never guaranteed.
Do I need trading experience to join?
No. Our managed service is fully hands-off. We handle everything — broker setup, strategy execution, risk management, and regular performance reporting.
What brokers do you work with?
We work with GTCFX, TradersHub Ltd, and UEXO — all regulated, tier-1 brokers. During onboarding we recommend the most suitable for your location and capital size.
